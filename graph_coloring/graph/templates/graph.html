VERTEX COLORING

A graphical solution


  • Instructions

      Draw a graph

      *Doubleclick on the canvas to draw a vertex.

      *Click on the first vertex and drag the mouse pointer to the second vertex to draw an edge between them.

      *To clear the canvas click on "clear" button.

      Note
      *If you are using chrome browser, then just give a single click on vertex before drawing edges.

      *Maximum of 10 colors allowed.
